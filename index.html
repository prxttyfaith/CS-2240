PROFILE

CHARLES LECLERC ON THE PERFECT TRAJECTORY

Full Name: Charles Leclerc
Birthdate: October 16, 1997
Birthplace: Monte Carlo, Monaco
Height: 1.80m
Weight: 69kg
Team: Scuderia Ferrari
Car Number: 16

Charles has displayed exceptional talent and intelligence throughout his motorsport career. He entered Formula 1 in 2018 with Alfa Romeo Sauber F1 and joined Scuderia Ferrari in 2019 alongside Sebastian Vettel. Leclerc's karting achievements include being the French Champion in 2009 and winning the Monaco Kart Cup in 2010, among others.

Transitioning to single-seaters in 2014, he quickly excelled, becoming the top rookie in Formula Renault and Formula 3. In 2016, he won the GP3 Series, and in 2017, he dominated the FIA Formula 2 season. Spotted by Nicolas Todt in 2010, Leclerc joined the Ferrari Driver Academy in 2016 and made his Formula 1 debut with Alfa Romeo Sauber.

In 2019, driving the Ferrari SF 90, he achieved 7 pole positions, 10 podiums, and 2 victories, finishing fourth in the World Championship. His contract with Ferrari was extended until 2024. The 2020 season fell below expectations, with only two podiums and an 8th-place finish. In 2021, Leclerc secured two pole positions and a podium.
